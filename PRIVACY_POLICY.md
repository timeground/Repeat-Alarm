# Privacy Policy for Repeat Reminder

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-04

**1. Introduction**
Welcome to Repeat Reminder. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

**2. Data Collection**
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. All data (such as alarm times and preferences) is stored locally on your device.

**3. Permissions**
The app requires the following permissions to function:
*   **Post Notifications:** To show alarm notifications.
*   **Schedule Exact Alarms:** To trigger alarms at the precise time you set.
*   **Vibrate:** To vibrate the device when an alarm goes off.
*   **Wake Lock:** To ensure the alarm fires even if the device is sleeping.

**4. Third-Party Services**
We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

**5. Changes to This Policy**
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

**6. Contact Us**
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
